Chad Knowlden

Licensed Massage Therapist & Instructor

Chad is a Licensed Massage Therapist and instructor with nearly 20 years of experience. Currently based at Bodymechanics' Vancouver campus, Chad is trained in multiple modalities, including Sports and Thai massage. He has extensive experience teaching Thai floor routines and is now thrilled to offer instruction in Thai techniques adapted for the massage table.

Throughout his career, Chad has worked alongside chiropractors and owned both a massage school and CEU business, bringing a wealth of knowledge and practical experience to his teaching. His approach emphasizes proper body mechanics and effective techniques that therapists can immediately incorporate into their practice.

Jebediah Sides

Licensed Massage Therapist

Deep TissueMyofascial ReleaseThai Massage

A former Army Ranger, Jebediah found his true calling in helping others achieve wellness and relief through therapeutic massage. A graduate of Bodymechanics School of Myotherapy and Massage, he has studied Vibration Massage and Thai stretch/massage techniques. He incorporates body mechanics and proprioceptive neuromuscular facilitation (PNF) to enhance recovery and overall well-being.

Jebediah is currently pursuing his NASM Personal Trainer Certification with specializations in Performance Enhancement and Corrective Exercise. This additional training allows him to offer a comprehensive approach to wellness that addresses both immediate therapeutic needs and long-term physical health.

His practice focuses on deep tissue, myofascial release, sports massage, and Thai techniques, with additional services including physiotherapy and vibration therapy.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At Bodywork Neuromuscular, our instructors share a common teaching philosophy centered on practical application, evidence-based techniques, and supportive learning environments. We believe that effective massage therapy education combines:

Hands-On Experience

We emphasize practical, hands-on learning that allows students to develop muscle memory and confidence in their techniques.

Scientific Understanding

Our courses are grounded in anatomy, physiology, and evidence-based practices to ensure therapists understand the "why" behind each technique.

Compassionate Care

We teach therapists to approach their work with empathy and presence, recognizing the importance of the therapeutic relationship.

Sustainable Practice

We focus on body mechanics and self-care strategies that help therapists maintain long, healthy careers without burnout or injury.
